Hello,

my name is Arthur Xavier and I'm a developer, a 20 years old Computer Science student at the Federal University of Minas Gerais in Brazil and an overall learner.

Excellence and passion are the keys to success and a happy professional life. Being able to make things happen is maybe the greatest virtue of the ones taking a shot in the technology branch and the opportunity of making an being the difference is what drives us all.

As an active self-taught developer with many years of experience I can say that coding is one of the greatest passions I have in my life, next to art, music and language learning.

I love getting to know and working with new technologies and trends. The state of the art is my art.

Node.JS, the new hype, is my favourite. I'm also deeply interested in design, web development, functional programming and all its capabilities, foremost, the ability to write simple yet powerful code, which one can easily verify and reason about.
